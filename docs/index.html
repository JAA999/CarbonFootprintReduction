THE BIGGER PICTURE

Our disregard for our carbon footprint and carelessness in the ways we contribute to greenhouse gas emissions every year has inevitably led to global warming and climate change (6). Global warming has had a massive impact on every aspect of life, from plants and animals to our own community.

While there have been numerous attempts to alert the public of the dangers of excessive carbon levels, they have chosen to feign ignorance and continue on with their daily lives despite the consequences.

However, there have been those that have aimed to diminish our carbon footprint at a grander scale with companies such as Tesla and organizations such as the Environmental Defense Fund (EDF). But for those wishing to aid in this mission, here are some tips and tricks to follow to help save our planet Earth.

HOW CAN WE DO OUR PART?

ALTER YOUR LIFESTYLE

  • Eat less meat or try alternatives such as fish and poultry.
  • Choose organic and locally sourced
  • Spend less on fast fashion and consider buying secondhand
  • Use a reusable bag when shopping or bring your own

TRANSPORT & ENERGY

  • Be concious of your fuel efficiency
  • If you have appliances over ten years old, onsider replacing them with Energy Star appliances
  • Consider other means of transportation for short distances such as walking or cycling
  • For longer trips, use cruise control to stabilize fuel consumption

OUR COMMUNITY

  • Vote for policies that protect the environment
  • Speak to a local representative
  • Invest in recycling bins
  • Switch to biodegradable products

Community Perspective

Recycle, Carpool, Take the bus (hard to do out here), renewable batteries. I think the best way to get your tips and tricks to be the most effective is picking stuff that is mostly viable (money and time wise) and easy to do. I think that is the biggest hurdle for going "more green." - David Hines
